Crash Course Weekend Venue

Important Note for Attendees: After you register for the event, we will hold a raffle to assign cabins. Each cabin will be randomly assigned, but you should form groups with other attendees ahead of time to ensure you room with your friends!

A group registration link will be distrubuted to everyone who has registered closer to the raffle time. Make sure to include the names of your group members when filling it out, and we will do our best to accommodate everyone together during the raffle!

All food will be self catered throughout the event by attendees

There is 1 assigned parking space per lodge but more parking avaliable in the local area

Total Capacity: 40-60 People

Cost per Person: £150-£200 per head
