About Crash Course Weekend

Welcome to Crash Course Weekend, a mini furry convention built with passion, dedication, and a deep love for the furry community. Located in the scenic landscapes of North Wales, our convention has been meticulously crafted to offer every attendee a luxery experience for lower cost.

Our main goal as a con is create a layed back, comfortable enviroment for attendees to enjoy a weekend away.

Whether you’re enjoying a peaceful nature walk, participating in partys, or unwinding at one of our exclusive hot tubs lodges, we aim to provide an environment where everyone feels welcome and comfortable. Our team has poured their hearts into making this event as inclusive and accessible as possible, so that everyone can enjoy the con.

With a focus on community, good vibes, and professionalism, we promise to deliver a furry weekend unlike any other — a place where you can be yourself and enjoy the beauty of North Wales.

Our Weekend Experience

Arrive on Friday afternoon and immerse yourself in the comfortable lodges.

On Sunday, we bid farewell with a heartwarming brunch, sending everyone off with a head full of memories.
