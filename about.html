About Crash Course Weekend

Welcome to Crash Course Weekend, a mini furry convention built with passion, dedication, and a deep love for the furry community. Nestled in the scenic landscapes of North Wales, our convention has been meticulously crafted to offer every attendee a luxery experience for low cost.

At Crash Course Weekend, we believe that a convention is more than just a gathering — it's an opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, make lasting friendships, and create cherished memories. From our cozy luxury eco-lodges to a weekend full of fun, we've curated every detail to ensure the perfect balance of relaxation, adventure, and creativity.

Whether you’re enjoying a peaceful nature walk, participating in partys, or unwinding at one of our exclusive hot tubs lodges, we aim to provide an environment where everyone feels welcome. Our team has poured their hearts into making this event as inclusive and accessible as possible, so that everyone can enjoy the full spectrum of activities.

With a focus on community, comfort, and creativity, we promise to deliver a furry weekend unlike any other — a place where you can be yourself and enjoy the beauty of North Wales.
