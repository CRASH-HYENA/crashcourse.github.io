Crash Course Weekend Convention Policy

1. Registration and Entry

Age Requirement: All attendees must be 18 years of age or older. A valid, current photo ID must be presented at registration to verify age. Accepted forms include passports, driving licenses, and other government-issued IDs.

Ticket Transfers and Refunds: Registrations can be transferred to another individual before the registration period closes. Cancellations are eligible for a full refund if requested within 7 days of payment. After this period, refunds will only be considered in exceptional circumstances.

2. Code of Conduct

Respect and Behaviour: Attendees are expected to treat others, including staff and fellow attendees, with respect. Discriminatory or disruptive behaviour will not be tolerated.

Fursuits and Costumes: Costumes are encouraged but must adhere to safety guidelines. Fursuits and related accessories must be worn responsibly.

Alcohol and Intoxication: Responsible consumption of alcohol is expected. Overly intoxicated individuals may be asked to leave the event to ensure the safety and comfort of all attendees and may receive a ban if they become disorderly or aggressive towards attendees or staff.

3. Venue Guidelines

Accommodation: The event will be held in luxury eco-lodges. Attendees are responsible for maintaining their cabins in perfect condition. Any damages will be charged to the occupant.

Quiet Hours: Please respect quiet hours from midnight to 8 AM to ensure a restful environment for all guests.

Smoking and Vaping: Smoking and vaping are permitted only in designated areas. The use of illegal substances is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate expulsion from the event and a lifetime ban.

Health Precautions: If you feel unwell during the event, notify a staff member. We encourage all attendees to take necessary health precautions, especially in group settings.

5. Accessibility

Support for Attendees with Disabilities: We aim to make the event as accessible as possible. If you have specific needs, please notify us in advance so we can make appropriate accommodations, such as accessible cabins or priority seating in event areas.

6. Pets

Bringing Pets: Pets are allowed but must be registered in advance. Owners are responsible for their pets at all times, and any damage caused by pets will be charged to the owner.
