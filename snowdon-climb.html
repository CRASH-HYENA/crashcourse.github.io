Snowdon Climbs

Every year, we organize an exciting weekend where a group of furries climb Mount Snowdon, with fursuit heads in tow to take memorable pictures at the top! The event is a fantastic blend of adventure, good vibes and furry shenanigans. It's a weekend-long activity and a chance to enjoy nature while having a unique experience.
